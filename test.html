Rural Village Center Urgency Index

The Urgency Index for a Rural Village Center Analyzes Zipcodes of Rural North Carolina for their ability to help achieve 4 Goals:

  1. Address Access Gaps in Health and Wellness

    • Adults reporting Poor or Fair General Health
    • Low Presence of Health Facilities

  2. Catalyze Housing Density & Value

    • High Presence of Primary and Secondary Schools
    • High Presence of Nature Parks

  3. Strengthen Community Cohesion

    • Limited 501(c)(3) organizations available to people
    • Households without Internet Access

  4. Spark Entrepreneurship & Innovation

    • High % of businesses are small businesses
    • High Presence of Junior Colleges, Technical Institutes, Vocational Schools, or Occupational Training
